What aspects of the DevOps course delivery have been most and least effective in enhancing your understanding and skills?

The hands on exercises are the most effective in enhancing my understanding and skills.
The least effective in enhancing my understanding was the course material, because it could have more important information and be more detailed, since that we use them to study.

Please list the 5 most interesting and the 5 least interesting aspects of the DevOps course.

5 most interesting aspects of the DevOps course:

  • Understanding the importance of having the DevOps tasks automated;
  • The hands on exercises to build pipelines in Jenkins and deploy applications in Firebase, also using Docker;
  • Being aware of some tools used to implement the DevOps process;
  • Understanding the benefits that DevOps brings to the projects;
  • Understanding how to do some of the DevOps tasks.

5 least interesting aspects of the DevOps course:

  • The course material that could be more detailed;
  • We could have more hands on exercises using some other tools mentioned, such as Terraform;
  • Problems when working with VMs;
  • One lab was difficult to follow because it was missing commands and instructions;
  • It would be more interesting if we had hands on exercises to build pipeline to run Tests, since that is a QA course.
